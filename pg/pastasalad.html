Spicy Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • Angel hair pasta
  • Flat leaf parsley, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 5 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp chilli flakes
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Method:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water.
  2. Put the mince garlic, olive oil, lemon juice and zest into a large bowl.
  3. Add the cooked pasta, some pasta water and mix.
  4. Add the parsley, chilli flakes, and more pasta water if needed.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Once the pasta has slightly cooled down, combine the cheese.
