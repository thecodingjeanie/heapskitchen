Wholewheat Wrap Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 large wholewheat wrap
  • 1/2 tin tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 white onion, thinly sliced
  • Parmesan cheese and truffle cheese, grated
  • Arulgua
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F
  2. Place the wholewheat wrap on a screaming pan, 1 minute each side
  3. Make the pasta sauce, fry the garlic in some olive oil, salt and pepper. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, some water and reduce.
  4. Add the pizza sauce, white onions and truffle cheese to the wrap and place in the oven.
  5. Once cooked, add the arugula, grated parmesan cheese and a drizzle of balsamic vinegar.
